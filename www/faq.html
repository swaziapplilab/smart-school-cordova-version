Frequent Asked Questions

1. Why should my child use Eswatini Smart School?

Because it will help your child improve his performance at school. It helps revising what is learnt at school

2. Which grades does it cover?

Eswatini Smart School covers from Grade 0 to form 5

3. How should I open an account for my child?

Just click on the green button START NOW and Sign up as a learner

4. Will my account need to be approved before I start using it?

Yes, the activation of the account takes between one and 24 hours after receiving the payment

5. How much does it cost to get an account?

Our promotional price is E100 / year for a learner and E200 / year for a Teacher

6. Will parent need an account?

No they do not need one. They can use their child account to check progress and communicate with teachers

7. Can a school request accounts for its pupils?

We encourage that. A school can request a bulk account for its pupils in getting in touch with us

8. Do you provide training worshop for teachers?

Yes we do provide training worshops to help teachers how to use those educational ressources

9. Do you have a youtube channel?

Yes we do, it is named ESWATINI SMART SCHOOL

Can a parent request a topic to be covered?

Yes, a parent can request a topic for his child where the child needs attention. Parents should contact us or send us an email
